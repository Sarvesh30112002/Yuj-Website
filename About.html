ABOUT OUR INSTITUTE

Vishwakarma Institute of Technology is an autonomous engineering college located in Pune. It was established in 1983 and is affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University. VIT became the first private college in Maharashtra to gain academic autonomy in the year 2008-09. Vishwakarma Institute of Technology has been awarded an A++ rank by NAAC, it ranks 10 in the Top Private Engineering Institutes Ranking in the country and 6 in West Zone Rankings according to the Times Engineering Institute Ranking Survey 2020. Welcome to YUJ

YUJ club focuses on promoting health and skill-building among students. The club takes its name from the Sanskrit word for yoga, which means to join or unite, reflecting its mission to bring together students to prioritize and enhance their well-being. Through various interesting activities, YUJ aims to help students develop and maintain healthy habits, cultivate skills and knowledge, and contribute to the betterment of society. Overall, YUJ club seeks to inspire and empower students to become more mindful, resilient, and engaged members of their communities.We encourage you to join the club and start your journey towards a healthier and happier life.
